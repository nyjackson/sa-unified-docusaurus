## Description
 A Licensed Home Care Services Agency offers home care services to clients who pay privately or have private insurance coverage. These types of agencies may also contract to provide services to Medicare/Medicaid beneficiaries whose cases are managed by another provider or entity. 

S & A Unified Inc., our company, is a LHCSA that provides home health aides and personal care aid services to all members.
