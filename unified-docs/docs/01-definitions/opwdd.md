## Definition 
The New York State Office for People with Development Disabilities is responsible for coordinating services for New Yorkers with developmental disabilities. These include intellectual disabilities, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, autism spectrum disorders, Prader-Willi syndrome and other neurological impairments. The services are provided directly and through a network of approximately 450 nonprofit service providing agencies.
