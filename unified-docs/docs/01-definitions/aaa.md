## Definition
An area agency on aging (AAA) is a public or private nonprofit agency designated by a state to address the needs and concerns of all older persons at the regional and local levels. 

AAAs are responsible for a geographic area, that is either a city, a single county, or a multi-county district. They coordinate and offer services that help older adults remain in their homes (if preferred), aided by services like, home-delivered meals, homemaker assistance, and any other service that ensures viable independent living.
