## Description
The Coordinator Team is the operational heart of the agency, responsible 
for matching caregivers with patients, 
ensuring EVV compliance, and managing daily 
schedule changes.

## Key Deadlines

Tuesday @ Noon (Prebiling)

## Core Responsibilities

- Managing shift overlaps and unassigned visits in HHAeXchange.

- Communicating with Patient Proxies regarding schedule changes.

- Monitoring expiring compliance documents (Annual Physicals, In-Services).

## Specific Responsibilites

### 1. Prebilling (The Tuesday Sprint)

Weekly reconciliation of the entire schedule for the previous week.

- **Goal**: Clear all "Schedule Exceptions" (e.g., visits without clock-ins, hours exceeding authorization).
- **Deadline**: Must be finished by **Tuesday** to allow the Payroll team to process checks.
- **Impact**: If this isn't done, caregivers don't get paid, and the agency can't invoice the insurance plans.

### 2. Dashboard Management

Daily "triage" of the agency's real-time status via the **[[HHAeXchange]]** dashboard.

- **Missed In**: Identifying caregivers who haven't clocked in within the 15-minute grace period.
- **Message Handling**: Responding to alerts from insurance care managers (NCMs).

### 3. Quality Calls (Retention)

Conducting proactive monthly check-ins with patients.

- **Purpose**: To verify they are happy with their caregiver and to catch service issues before the patient decides to switch agencies.

### 4. Conflict Resolution (Overlaps)

Handling "Overlaps"—logistical disputes where another agency claims to be providing care at the same time as S & A Unified.

- **Action**: Reviewing EVV data to prove our caregiver was physically present.
