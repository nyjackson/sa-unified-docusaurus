## Responsibilities
- Aids clients in applying for medical services and supplies
- Conducts daily meetings with Home and Community Support Services Agencies (HCSSA) and documents Individual Service Reports (ISR)
- Visits patients in-person 3 times a year and calls every month to check-in on health and care status. 
- Conducts yearly Nursing Home Transition and Diversion Medicaid Waiver renewal application for each client.
